Summary


My interest in Computer programming was triggered after I started playing around on a friend's computer in 1987. I started small by purchasing a Bondwell 64K computer on which I taught myself GW Basic programming. I knew immediately that this was what I want to do in life. I'm already 30+ years in the information technology industry and enjoy it more than ever before.




I have worked in car manufacturing systems, retail banking, bank trade, national payments, ATM banking, home loans, consumer payments, automatic payments, Credit & Master Card, International systems, universal exchange, customer information systems etc. I am a strong team player and able to work independently. I am continuously looking to learn and increase my skill set.




Hire me and you will have an excellent problem solver, a fast learner and a loyal and dedicated employee who will be a great asset to your company. I'm the type of person who does what I do because I have a great love and passion for it and not just because I want money for it, therefore I thrive on challenges and am eager to learn more due to our fast-growing technological industry.
